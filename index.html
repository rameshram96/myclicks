How to add a photo to the gallery

  1. Rename your photo using this format:
    Title__Location__YYYY-MM-DD.jpg
    e.g. Morning_Mist__Western_Ghats__2024-11-20.jpg
  2. Go to github.com/rameshram96/myclicks → photos folder
  3. Click "Add file" → "Upload files"
  4. Drop your renamed photo and click "Commit changes"
  5. Refresh this page — your photo appears with title, location & date ✓

💡 Use _ for spaces within each part, and __ (double underscore) to separate the three parts.
All three parts are optional — Sunset.jpg or Sunset__Goa.jpg both work fine too.
